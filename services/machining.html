Service Capabilities

Built for fast recovery and dependable production support.

We help maintenance and operations teams reduce downtime with practical machining, on-site service, and equipment restoration.

Machining & Hydraulics

Custom fabrication, part modification, and equipment repair led by Red Seal machinists. We handle steel, aluminum, exotic alloys, and plastics.

  • Lathe services — up to 52" swing, 10ft length
  • Milling, drilling, boring, and shaping
  • Hydraulic cylinder repair & testing to 3,500 psi
  • CAD design & reverse engineering
  • CNC plasma cutting — 5'x10' bed, 6" capacity

Field Service

On-site mechanical support to identify issues quickly and stabilize critical equipment with advanced diagnostics.

  • Vibration analysis and thermal imaging
  • Laser shaft alignment
  • Laser belt alignment
  • Emergency support for downtime events
  • Maintenance support and job follow-through

Pump Rebuilds

Comprehensive pump repair including teardown, internal and external inspection, bearing replacement, mechanical seal replacement, and rotor balancing.

  • Impeller concentricity inspection
  • Surface treatment and verification
  • Circulating, condensate, and fire pumps
  • Gear, submersible, vacuum, and vertical turbine pumps

Fabrication & Rigging

Equipment restoration with in-house fabrication capabilities. Certified equipment removal, relocation, and reinstallation.

  • MIG, TIG, and stick welding
  • Aluminum spool gun work
  • Oxide blasting, paint & epoxy application
  • 3-ton & 6-ton knuckle boom cranes, up to 65ft reach
  • Compressors, gearboxes, blowers, and motors
